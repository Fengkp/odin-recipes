Honey Garlic Chicken

Description:

Easy to make Honey Garlic Chicken recipe. Just prep and throw into a slow cooker.

The ingredients are simple to acquire and leaves this fatty piece of chicken golden brown and juicy.

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

    Step 1

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.

    2. Step 2

  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in in a bowl; pour over the chicken.

    3. Step 3

  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
